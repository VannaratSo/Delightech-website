How It Works

4 Easy Steps To Get Instant Loan And Repayment

Step 1

Download App & Submit Application with simple required details.

Only 7min for filling application and what require to be prepare 1. National Identification Card 2. Personal Telephone Number 3. Income Proof Documents

Step 2

Quick Result with instant approval for your loan application.

Keep monitoring and get a quick result on your mobile phone dashboard then it’s your turn to decide to accept a loan or not.

Step 3

Direct Transfer to your bank account once approved.

Amount of disbursement will transfer directly to your pocket with our payment channels which next to you and countrywide less than 30min

Step 4

Convenient Repayment options with flexible scheduling.

You can choose payment option which fit with you and select variety of payment channel which available anywhere and next to you
